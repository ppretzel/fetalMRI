Patient Positioning

Setup and coil placement for fetal MRI

Position
  • Supine position preferred throughout pregnancy — start supine even in the third trimester; switch to lateral decubitus only when supine becomes untenable
  • Lateral decubitus is suboptimal: the fetus is farther from the coil and has more space to move; if unavoidable, position the coil laterally rather than anteriorly
Coil & Patient Setup
  • Legs slightly flexed over a foam wedge
  • Arms resting on the chest with a small pad underneath
  • Coil placed directly on the abdomen — no plexiglass plate between patient and coil
